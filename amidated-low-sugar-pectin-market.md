<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Amidated Low Sugar Pectin market primarily comprises two types: High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin and Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin. HM Pectin requires higher sugar concentrations and is ideal for producing gels, especially in traditional jams and jellies. In contrast, LM Pectin can gel with lower sugar levels or even in the presence of calcium, making it suitable for low-sugar and sugar-free products. Both types cater to diverse food applications, balancing textural properties and consumer preferences for healthier options.</p></p>
