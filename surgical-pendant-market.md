<p><p>The surgical pendant market is categorized into two main types: electrical and mechanical. Electrical surgical pendants integrate advanced technologies for seamless power supply and connectivity, enabling efficient operation of surgical instruments and lighting systems. They often include built-in sockets and communication ports. In contrast, mechanical surgical pendants rely on physical support structures for equipment, offering flexibility and stability without electrical components. Both types are essential in operating rooms, enhancing workflow and optimizing surgical environments for improved patient care.</p></p>
