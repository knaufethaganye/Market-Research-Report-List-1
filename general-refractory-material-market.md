<p><p>The general refractory material market is categorized into three main types: acid refractories, neutral refractories, and basic refractories. Acid refractories, resistant to acidic environments, are ideal for industries like glass and ceramics. Neutral refractories, exhibiting stable properties in various conditions, are commonly used in furnace linings. Basic refractories, which can withstand basic slag and alkaline materials, are essential in industries such as steel production. Each type serves specific industrial needs based on their chemical resistance and thermal stability.</p></p>
