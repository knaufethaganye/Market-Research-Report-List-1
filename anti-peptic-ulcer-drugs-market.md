<p><strong>The Anti Peptic Ulcer Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs)</li><li>Potassium-Competitive Acid Blockers (P-CAB)</li><li>Antacids</li><li>H2 Antagonists</li><li>Antibiotics</li><li>Ulcer Protective Drugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-peptic ulcer drugs market consists of several key types. Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs) reduce stomach acid production, offering potent relief from ulcers. Potassium-Competitive Acid Blockers (P-CAB) also inhibit acid secretion but through a different mechanism. Antacids neutralize existing stomach acid for quick relief, while H2 Antagonists block histamine receptors to decrease acid production. Antibiotics are used to eliminate H. pylori infection, a common ulcer cause, and Ulcer Protective Drugs, like sucralfate, create a protective barrier on ulcer surfaces.</p></p>
