<p><p>Solar water heaters (SWH) are systems that harness solar energy to heat water for residential and commercial use. The two main market types are compact and split systems. Compact type SWH integrates all components, including solar collectors and storage tanks, into a single unit, making it easier to install and more space-efficient. In contrast, split type systems separate the collectors and storage tank, allowing for versatile installation options and better performance in varying weather conditions. Both serve to enhance energy efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
