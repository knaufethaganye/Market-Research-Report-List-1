<p><strong>The Oil-less Scroll Vacuum Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-sided Scroll</li><li>Double-sided Scroll</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil-less scroll vacuum pumps market is segmented into single-sided and double-sided scroll types. Single-sided scroll pumps use one spiral mechanism to compress gas, making them simpler and more compact, suitable for applications requiring moderate vacuum levels. In contrast, double-sided scroll pumps utilize two interleaved spirals, allowing for higher capacities and lower ultimate pressures, ideal for demanding industrial applications. Both types offer efficient, low-maintenance performance, as they eliminate oil contamination, enhancing their appeal in various sectors.</p></p>
