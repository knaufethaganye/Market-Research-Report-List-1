<p><p>Push Back Racks are a type of pallet rack system designed to improve warehouse efficiency by allowing for high-density storage and easy retrieval of stored items. This system utilizes a series of nested carts that slide along inclined rails, enabling pallets to be pushed back when loading and then automatically positioned at the front for easy access when unloading. This design maximizes storage capacity while reducing aisle space requirements and enhancing order picking efficiency.</p><p>The Push Back Rack Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.1% during the forecast period.
