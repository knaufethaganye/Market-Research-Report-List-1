<p><p>The paper drying system market encompasses various technologies, including rolling bed dryers, which utilize a moving belt to transport paper while evaporating moisture. Air dryers employ heated air circulation to accelerate drying. Convection dryers use heated air currents to promote moisture removal, while conduction dryers transfer heat through direct contact with the paper surface. Other drying systems may include technologies like infrared or microwave drying. Each type caters to different production needs, efficiency levels, and paper characteristics.</p></p>
