<p><p>Centralized Automatic Lubrication Systems are essential for maintaining machinery efficiency by delivering precise amounts of lubricant. In the Grease Lubrication System, lubricant is delivered in a thick, semi-solid form, ideal for high-load applications where retention and resistance to leakage are critical. Conversely, the Oil Lubrication System uses a fluid lubricant, suitable for high-speed operations that require efficient heat dissipation and lower friction. Both systems aim to reduce downtime and maintenance costs while enhancing overall equipment performance.</p></p>
